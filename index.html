Our Services

We have a wide array of services for individuals and businesses.

International Shipping

Ship packages, documents, or cargo to and from Jamaica to destinations around the world with ease. We Ship It Ja partners with trusted global carriers to ensure fast and secure deliveries.

Delivery & Courier Service

Need something delivered across town or to another parish? Our local courier team handles pickups and same-day or next-day deliveries anywhere in Jamaica. From business documents to retail orders, we guarantee timely, reliable, and trackable service.

Freight & Cargo Management

For large or bulk shipments, We Ship It Ja provides sea and air freight solutions customized to your timeline and budget. We handle everything from packaging and warehousing to logistics coordination and customs clearance.

About Us

At 'We Ship It Ja', we believe shipping should be simple, reliable, and built around you. Whether you're a small business sourcing goods from overseas or a family waiting on a care package, we make global logistics feel local.

We’re a Caribbean-born shipping company with a global reach. Our mission is to bridge the gap between international commerce and regional convenience — giving individuals and businesses access to the world, one package at a time.

We provide secure, affordable, and transparent shipping solutions from the U.S. to the Caribbean. From package forwarding and consolidation to customs clearance and last-mile delivery, we handle the logistics so you don’t have to.
